With the continuous evolution and complexity of healthcare terms, students, nurses, nursing technicians, and other healthcare professionals need a quick, accessible, compact, and comprehensive reference work. To clarify and simplify the language of health sciences, we have developed the Medical and Nursing Term Dictionary.
