About

Welcome to the Wildlife Conservation Alliance! Our organization is dedicated to the protection and conservation of endangered wildlife species around the world. We believe that every species has a critical role to play in the ecosystem, and that it is our responsibility to protect and preserve them for future generations. We are proud to be supported by a team of dedicated conservationists, scientists, and community organizers who bring a wealth of expertise and experience to our work. We are also grateful for the support of our partners and donors, who make our conservation efforts possible. If you share our passion for wildlife conservation, we invite you to get involved with our organization. Whether through membership, volunteering, or donations, your support can make a real difference in protecting endangered species and preserving the natural world.



Our Mission

Our mission is to work with local communities, governments, and other conservation organizations to develop sustainable conservation solutions that benefit both wildlife and people. We do this through a combination of research, advocacy, and hands-on conservation efforts in the field.

Our History

Since our founding in 1995, we have worked tirelessly to protect a wide range of species, including elephants, rhinos, tigers, and gorillas. Our projects have included anti-poaching initiatives, habitat protection and restoration, and community outreach programs designed to educate and empower local communities to become advocates for wildlife conservation.
